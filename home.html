About DSIISC

Delhi, as the capital of India, reflects the spirit and diversity of the entire nation. Since its establishment in February 1971, the Delhi State Industrial and Infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd. (DSIIDC) has been a driving force in shaping the city’s growth and infrastructure. Over the years, DSIIDC has actively planned, supported, financed, and executed projects that have transformed Delhi into a hub of industry, commerce, and inclusive urban development.

Today, DSIIDC continues to carry forward this responsibility with several landmark projects in progress, including Knowledge-Based Industrial Park (KBI), Baprola, Built-up Factory Complex, Rani Khera, Affordable Housing Projects, Educational Infrastructure and many more.
